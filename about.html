About Us

Precision, performance, and passion — built from decades of experience.

Our Story

At Island Boyz Performance, we live and breathe high-performance cars — especially Hondas. With over 35 years of hands-on experience working directly with Honda vehicles, our journey began in major auto service centers, where we fine-tuned our skills and built a reputation for honest, reliable, and precise work.

After decades of turning wrenches and tuning motors for someone else, we knew it was time to carve our own path. We opened Island Boyz Performance to pursue our lifelong dream of creating a garage where quality, culture, and craftsmanship come first. We take pride in our roots, our experience, and our commitment to helping fellow car lovers bring out the best in their builds.

Whether it’s a full motor swap, custom dyno tuning, or just making sure your car runs like new — our mission is simple: treat every build like it’s our own.
